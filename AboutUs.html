TAILORS OF THE TIDE

MEET THE MAKERS


Rooted in the Philippines since 2024, our team of twenty-four skilled tailors share the same obsession: the ocean. With precision, care, and dedication, they bring each garment to life, creating seaman clothing that seafearers can rely on throughout their journeys.

01. THE MASTER CUTTER

These are the experts who start the process. They have the strength and skill to use heavy cutting machines with total control. It takes a sharp eye and years of practice to cut thick fabrics perfectly. Because of their hard work, every piece of clothing starts with the right shape and size.

02. TEAMWORK

Our workshop is filled with people who have made sewing their life’s work. This team works together to make sure every garment is made correctly. They don’t just run machines; they use their shared experience to solve problems and help one another. The quality of our products comes from the strong teamwork of these makers.

03. DEDICATION

These makers are defined by their intense focus and patience. You can see their dedication in the way they handle small, intricate tasks and difficult sewing patterns. They have the visual accuracy needed to spot errors and the steady hands required to reinforce every seam for the person wearing it.

04. PASSION

They are the type of people who treat their work as a craft rather than just a job. By using their eyes and hands to guide every machine, they provide a level of quality that automation cannot match. Their skill and professional pride are what make our clothing reliable for the most demanding environments.
