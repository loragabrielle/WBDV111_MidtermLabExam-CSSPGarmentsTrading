Our
Journey.

What started as a small family-run business supplying Coveralls to the maritime community. Today, we’ve grown into a trusted partner for crews and offshore rigs across the Philippines, but we still operate with the heart and hustle of a small shop that values real craftsmanship.

We are obsessed with the details, hand-selecting every raw material—from the high-grip soles of our Safety Shoes to the heat-resistant fabric of our Chef Tops. Our team puts in the hard work to ensure every seam is strong and every fastener is secure, because we know that out at sea, there is no room for gear that fails.

We don’t just ship boxes; we provide a Commitment to the men and women working the decks. Having sailed those same waters ourselves, we understand the stakes, which is why we work tirelessly to deliver durable PPE that protects you through every storm and every shift.
