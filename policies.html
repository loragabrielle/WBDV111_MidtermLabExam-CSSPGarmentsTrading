Shipping Policy

Local Delivery

We provide nationwide coverage across the Philippine archipelago. Our logistics partners ensure your gear arrives safely whether you are in a major city or a coastal province.

  • Metro Manila & Greater Luzon: 2–3 Business Days
  • Visayas & Mindanao Provinces: 5–7 Business Days
  • Remote Island Locations: 7–10 Business Days

International Shipping

CSSP Garments Trading serves the global maritime community. We ship to major international hubs for seafarers and culinary professionals all over the world.

  • Standard International Shipping: 7–14 Business Days
  • Express Global Shipping: 3–5 Business Days
  • Note: Customs duties and local taxes are the responsibility of the recipient.

1. Order Processing & Craftsmanship

Unlike mass-produced apparel, every order from CSSP Garments is handled with precision.

Standard Processing: Please allow 1–2 business days for quality inspection and packaging.
Custom/Bulk Orders: For customized maritime uniforms or bulk gear for crews, production lead times typically range from 7–14 days depending on the volume and design complexity. Our team in the Antipolo workshop ensures every stitch meets maritime standards before dispatch.

2. Shipping Rates & Calculations

Shipping costs are calculated based on the total weight of your parcel and the destination. You can view the exact shipping fee at the checkout page before finalizing your purchase. These rates are live-synced with our courier partners to provide the most accurate and competitive pricing available.

Promotional Offer: Keep an eye out for our "Free Shipping" banners on the site ticker. These apply to select domestic orders exceeding a specific minimum spend, providing an excellent opportunity for crews to consolidate their gear requirements and save on logistical costs.

3. Shipment Tracking & Transparency

Once your order leaves our workshop, transparency is our priority. You will receive an automated notification (Email or SMS) containing your Unique Tracking Number and a direct link to the courier's tracking portal. This system allows you to monitor your package from the moment it leaves our Antipolo facility until it reaches your doorstep. Please allow 24 hours for the tracking information to update once the package is scanned by the carrier, as local sorting centers may require time to synchronize their digital logs.

4. Delivery Attempts & Failed Deliveries

Our couriers typically make two (2) delivery attempts to ensure you receive your maritime gear. We highly recommend providing an active mobile number and a clear landmark for your address to assist our riders in locating you quickly. If the recipient is unavailable, the package may be returned to our hub. Redelivery fees may apply in cases of incorrect addresses provided by the customer or repeated failed pickup attempts, so please double-check your shipping details before placing an order.

5. Damaged or Missing Parcels

In the rare event that your gear is damaged during transit or if a parcel is declared lost, CSSP Garments Trading provides full support and a streamlined resolution process. We prioritize the safety of your order, but we acknowledge that logistical challenges can occasionally occur during long-distance transit. We recommend taking a video while unboxing your package ("Unboxing Proof") to expedite any claims or replacement requests within 48 hours of receipt. This clear documentation helps us hold our logistics partners accountable and ensures that we can send out a replacement as quickly as possible.

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

At CSSP Garments Trading, we only collect information that is strictly necessary to fulfill your orders and provide a personalized experience. This includes your name, shipping address, contact number, and email address for transactional purposes. For custom maritime gear, we may also keep records of specific measurements provided during the design process to ensure the perfect fit for your crew. Additionally, we may document corporate logo preferences and embroidery specifications to maintain consistency across all your future uniform requirements.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data serves as the foundation for our entire production and fulfillment workflow at CSSP Garments Trading. We utilize these details to accurately process custom maritime orders within our Antipolo workshop, ensuring that every garment meets your specific technical requirements and embroidery standards. Beyond production, your information is essential for coordinating secure logistics and door-to-door deliveries, managing payment verifications, and providing real-time tracking updates to keep you informed of your shipment's progress. We also use your contact details to respond to technical inquiries and occasionally share exclusive updates regarding new maritime garment releases or seasonal industrial promotions. Furthermore, analyzing order history helps us optimize our inventory of high-quality materials to better serve the evolving needs of the seafaring community.

3. Data Security

We utilize advanced encryption and strict physical security protocols to safeguard your sensitive information from any unauthorized access. All payment transactions are handled through highly secure processing channels to ensure that your financial details are never stored on our local systems or servers. Your personal data is housed within protected environments with restricted access, which are constantly monitored to prevent potential cyber threats and vulnerabilities. To maintain the high level of trust essential to our operations, we perform consistent checks on our data practices, ensuring they always align with our commitment to security and excellence.

4. Compliance with Local Regulations

Our data handling practices are strictly aligned with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) of the Philippines. We ensure that all personal information processed in our Antipolo facility is handled with the highest level of confidentiality as required by national law, protecting both our local and international maritime partners. By adhering to these regulatory standards, we guarantee that your data is processed fairly, lawfully, and transparently at all times. This legal framework guides our commitment to upholding your privacy rights throughout every stage of our business operations.

5. Your Rights

You have the absolute right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, or to ask for its immediate correction or deletion from our records. If you wish to opt-out of marketing communications or newsletters, you may do so at any time by contacting our official support channels. We are committed to responding to all privacy-related requests promptly and will provide you with a clear explanation of how your data is being managed upon inquiry. Your control over your personal information remains a top priority as we strive to provide excellent service and high-quality maritime gear.
